About Florithm

Technology that enhances your everyday life

At Florithm, we believe great software should feel effortless. What started in 2019 as an independent iOS developer journey has grown into Florithm LLC — a dedicated mobile app studio creating and publishing applications across lifestyle, creative tools, and fashion tech.

Our mission is to design intuitive and engaging apps that empower users through innovative features like color analysis, digital styling, and user-generated content. With a focus on thoughtful design and smart technology, we strive to elevate your daily experiences and inspire creativity in everything you do.
