Privacy Policy — Fleet Keeper+

Effective Date: February 27, 2026  ·  Contact: contact@florithm.com

Fleet Keeper+ is designed to provide an engaging offline and multiplayer gaming experience while respecting your privacy.

1. Information Collection and Use

A. Personal Information

We do not require you to create an account and do not actively collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.

B. Game Data and Multiplayer

  • Multiplayer Server: Gameplay data (player positioning, score, achievement count) is transmitted to our matchmaking server in real-time and is not stored permanently.
  • Local Storage: Game progress, settings, and high scores are saved on your device only.

C. Third-Party Services

  • Game Center (Apple): Used for global leaderboards and achievements. Apple manages your player profile per their privacy policy.
  • Advertising (Unity LevelPlay): Ads are displayed after a certain number of deaths. Unity and its partners may collect device identifiers (IDFA), IP address, and usage data for ad delivery. See Unity Privacy Policy.

2. Analytics and Crash Reporting

We may collect anonymous crash logs and statistical usage data to diagnose issues and improve performance.

3. Children's Privacy

This app does not address anyone under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us immediately.

4. Security

We use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, though no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.

Questions? Contact us at contact@florithm.com
