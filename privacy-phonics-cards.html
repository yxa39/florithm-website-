Privacy Policy — Phonics Cards

Effective Date: March 1, 2026  ·  Contact: contact@florithm.com

Phonics Cards is an educational tool for learning English phonics. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

Local Data Only

Custom phonics sets and personal notes you create are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit this data to our servers.

No Account Required

No registration or personal information is needed to use the app.

2. Audio

Pronunciation audio is pre-recorded and bundled with the app. The app does not record your voice or access the microphone.

3. Third-Party Services

The app may use anonymous analytics and crash reporting to improve stability and performance. No personally identifiable information is collected.

4. In-App Purchases

The "Unlock Everything" purchase is handled securely by Apple. We do not store your payment information.

5. Children's Privacy

Phonics Cards may be used by children under parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.
