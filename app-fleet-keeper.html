About this app

Your fleet is your lifeline

Fleet Keeper+ is the ultimate space survival and arcade shooter where your fleet of ships is your health — lose your ships and it's game over.

Navigate treacherous asteroid fields, rescue drifting vessels to grow your fleet, and collect epic power-ups like Deflection Shields, Hearts, and Multi-Lasers to fight back.

Challenge real players in real-time multiplayer battles and climb the global Game Center leaderboards. Unlock achievements and prove you're the ultimate fleet commander.

Key Features

Fleet as Health

Your ships are your health bar — collect more to survive longer, lose them all and it's over.

Asteroid Fields

Navigate through increasingly dangerous asteroid fields as difficulty ramps up.

Rescue Vessels

Rescue drifting ships to grow your fleet and extend your survival.

Epic Power-Ups

Collect Deflection Shields, Hearts, Multi-Lasers, and more to turn the tide.

Real-Time Multiplayer

Battle real players from around the world in live multiplayer matches.

Global Leaderboards

Compete for the top spot on Game Center leaderboards and unlock achievements.
