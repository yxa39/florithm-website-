Privacy Policy — Fish or Bait

Effective Date: February 7, 2026  ·  Contact: contact@florithm.com

Fish or Bait is an iOS game. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

No account required. We do not ask for your name, email, or other personal information to play.

Ads

The game displays ads. Advertising partners may automatically collect information such as device identifiers (including IDFA if you allow tracking permission), IP address, and ad interaction data (impressions and clicks) to show ads, measure performance, and help prevent fraud.

2. How Information Is Used

Information collected by advertising partners is used to:

  • Deliver and measure ads
  • Limit repetitive ads
  • Detect and prevent fraud

3. Tracking Choices (iOS)

If the app requests permission under Apple's App Tracking Transparency, you can allow or deny tracking. If you deny, ads may be less personalized. You can change this anytime in iOS Settings.

4. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data may be shared with advertising partners only for displaying and measuring ads.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date will be updated above.
