About this app

Make getting things done rewarding

TapTapTask is a personal task planner designed to help you complete tasks, build habits, and achieve your goals — with a twist: you earn points for every task you finish.

The more tasks you complete, the more points you accumulate. Watch your score grow as your productivity improves, and track your progress with detailed graphs and tables.

Perfect for families and kids too — assign tasks, earn rewards, and build great habits together.

Key Features

Points & Rewards System

Earn points for every completed task. The more you do, the higher your score climbs.

Habit Building

Set recurring tasks to build positive daily habits over time.

Progress Tracking

Visualize your productivity with graphs and tables showing your task history.

Flexible Scheduling

Schedule one-time or recurring tasks with custom frequencies.

Notes & Details

Add notes and daily details to any task for full context.

Family Friendly

Great for families — assign tasks to kids and reward them for completing chores.
