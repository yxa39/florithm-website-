About this app

The right book at the right level

Do you want your kids to read more, but don't know which books are right for their level? RightRead solves that by organizing curated, 5-star books from Kindergarten through Grade 6.

Instead of overwhelming children with endless choices, the app presents just five carefully selected books at a time. Swipe down to discover more when they're ready.

Every book is hand-picked and 5-star rated so you can trust the quality. Reading levels are clearly labeled so parents and kids can find the perfect match.

Key Features

K–Grade 6 Reading Levels

Books organized by grade level from Kindergarten through Grade 6 for accurate leveling.

5-Star Curated Books

Every book is hand-selected and 5-star rated for quality and engagement.

Five Books at a Time

Presents a manageable selection of five books to prevent choice overload.

Swipe to Discover More

Swipe down to see a fresh batch of books when your child is ready for new titles.

Ratings & Reviews

Each book includes star ratings and user reviews to help parents decide.

Built for Parents

Makes it easy to find the right book without guesswork.
