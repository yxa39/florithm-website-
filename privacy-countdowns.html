Privacy Policy — Countdowns — Timers at a Glance

Effective Date: June 6, 2025  ·  Contact: contact@florithm.com

Countdowns is a productivity app for tracking deadlines and important dates. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

Local Data Only

All tasks, events, notes, and countdown data you enter are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit this data to our servers.

No Account Required

No registration is needed. No personal information is collected by us.

2. Third-Party Services

The app may use anonymous analytics or crash reporting to improve performance and fix bugs. These services do not identify you personally.

3. In-App Purchases

Subscriptions and purchases are managed by Apple. We do not access your payment information.

4. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.
